TECHSHED FROME

play.collobrate.make

A weekly communal space for the tech curious to play, collaborate and make.

What is techshed?

A group of technology enthusiasts that get together each week in social space to ‘tinker’... with everything from Arduinos, Rasberry Pi, soldering, programming, lasers, websites, apps, VR, 3d printers.

Working on projects

We identify problems by connecting to the town council and local businesses, to create interesting and technological solutions to that benefit the community.

Can I help out on a project?

See our a problem board that lists all the on-going projects.

If you think you can add something to it, from the marketing, to the design or the coding then you can buddy up with the team.

Can I work on my own thing and just tinker?

Yes! Whatever it is, if its your own interest, you want to just play and learn or you might have identified a new problem that technology can solve in the wider community.

Who should get involved?

We welcome everyone who’s interested in the community and using technology to make it a better place. Below are the sorts of useful roles that some members bring to Techshed Frome

Developers

Whatever you langauges is from PHP, Python, C++, Java, Ruby, Javascript, HTML, CSS we want it all.

Datanauts

We often find we are playing with data, so anyone that loves this and can lend a hand.

Organisers

You love to herd the sheep, work out what needs doing and make things happen.

Communicators

Communicate complex ideas in simple ways to any audience?

Designers

UXers, graphic or brand designers that can make things simple, easy and beautiful all at the same time.

Can I chat to someone before I come?

Sure why not chat to the team and we will do our best to make you feel at home

Will you fix my laptop or tech kit?

We don’t fix stuff on our own, but if you bring it along you may find another member can help or give you pointers. It can be your next project.

I don’t feel very confident about technology am I welcome?

Yes! Whilst we don’t offer lessons or any courses, if you are willing to try, mess it up and try again, a member will be happy to show you the basics. You might also be interested in coming to an open day

Join us or just pop in and say hello

We’re so glad you're interested in Techshed Frome. You’re welcome to just pop in and check it out for free, or sign up now.
