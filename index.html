What is Tech Shed Frome?

A group of technology enthusiasts that meet each week in social space to ‘tinker’. We believe that if we put people together, who love technology, we can make some useful things for the community.

What do you mean by technology?
Everything from Arduinos, Rasberry Pi's, drones, soldering, programming, lasers, websites, apps, VR, 3d printers.

What happens at Tech Shed Frome?

Weekly Sessions
We meet every wednesday from 7-9pm @ The Welish Mill Hub. This can to socialise, share, learn and create.

Open Build Days
Open build days are for anyone to join. For example we might have a 'Build an air auaility sensor workshop', to get more people interested in Tech. This will be a structured format and planned event.

Working on projects
We identify problems by connecting to the town council, businesses, and local people to create interesting and technological solutions that benefit the community.
Can I help out on a project?

See our problem board that lists all the on-going projects. If you think you can add something to it, from the marketing, to the design or the coding then you can buddy up with the team.




Give us a challenge!
If you think you've got a local community problem that needs fresh thinking, tell us or drop in any wednesday 7-9pm @Remakery:Frome.

Can I work on my own thing and just tinker?

Yes! Whatever it is, if its your own interest, or you want to just play and learn.

Who should get involved?

We welcome everyone who’s interested in the community and using technology to make it a better place.

Below are the sorts of useful roles that some members bring to Tech Shed Frome.

Developers
Whatever you langauges is from PHP, Python, C++, Java, Ruby, Javascript, HTML, CSS we want it all.

Organisers & Communicators
You love to herd the sheep, work out what needs doing and make things happen. Or perhaps you can communicate complex ideas in simple ways to any audience?

Designers
UXers, graphic or brand designers that can make things simple, easy and beautiful all at the same time.

Technologists
Maybe you just play with a bit of everything from electronics, programming, lasers, 3d printing.. whatever it's all welcome.
Can I chat to someone before I come?

Sure why not chat to the team and we will do our best to make you feel at home

Will you fix my laptop or tech kit?

We don’t fix stuff on our own, but if you bring it along you may find another member can help or give you pointers. It can be your next project.

I don’t feel very confident about technology am I welcome?

Yes! Whilst we don’t offer lessons or any courses, if you are willing to try, mess it up and try again, a member will be happy to show you the basics. You might also be interested in coming to an open day.
